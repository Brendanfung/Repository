About Me

My name is Brendan Fung and I am a student at SAIT under the IDD program. Some of my hobbies include writing music, going to live shows, and creating art. As a creatively driven individual my goal is to eventually make money selling my art. My favourite colour is red and my favourite food is a nice miso ramen. I have one sibling and a pet dog. Funnily enough I often find myself more annoyed by my human sibling instead of my pet dog.
